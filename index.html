The game will be based on a custom Fatespinner setting developed by iORC - a team of enthusiasts enticed by PnP RPGs. Gaming rules are based on a rulebook developed for this setting and were tested and balanced during the last 6 years of gaming experience. We plan to create a turn-based game with gameplay similar to offline world PnP gaming sessions. At starters playable races will be: humans, elves, dwarves, orcs, goblins, lapinoms, drakes, kobolds and beastmans. Most of races have bunch of nations with unique traits and abilities.

Fatespinner will provide two gaming modes: strategy and RPG. If you play strategy, you will manage territorial domains and run their economy and warfare. While playing in RPG mode your gaming experience will be based on opportunities created by domain managers. You can be hired by domain ruler with the system of indirect quests somewhat similar to Majesty. The driving force of this game will be political engagements between groups of players (clans): their warfare in strategy mode will create opportunities for players which play RPG.

FAQ

When will the game be released?
We plan to start beta in second half of 2014. After the beta we'll announce roadmap for release.
Which platforms do you target?
It will be browser game, so we target any platform which can run modern browser. We also plan to create mobile apps later on.
Will the game be free to play?
Definitely. We even don't plan to sell gaming currency and items. We have some innovative ideas for monetization which we'll announce later.
